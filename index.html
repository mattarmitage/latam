Can Regional Sovereign
Wealth Funds Expand and
Grow in Latin America?

Dr. Victoria Barbary

Prof. Patrick J Schena

When considering the global dispersion of sovereign wealth funds, particularly recently in Africa and South-East Asia, practitioners and academics share a common query:

Why has Latin America lagged in establishing sovereign investment institutions capable of complementing the work of finance ministries and central banks in managing state assets to promote macroeconomic stability, intergenerational savings, or strategic national development?
